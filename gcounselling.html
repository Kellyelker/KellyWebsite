Group Counselling

I am a single momma, a teacher, counsellor, volunteer, and a life-long learner. My passion is to help others on their own journey. I love to travel, eat beautiful food, and stay active. More than anything I love to share all of these things with the people in my life. As a teacher for the past 10 years I have had the incredible opportunity to teach hundreds of students. In this role I am able to support students to reach their educational goals. My experience has taught me that children often need more than this type of guidance on their journeys. They require social and emotional support in order to thrive. Therein lies the root of my desire to become a counsellor.
