About

I am a teacher, counsellor, world traveler, adventurer, and a life-long learner. I graduated from Wilfrid Laurier University with a double degree in Sociology and Geography and a minor in Psychology in 2012. The following year I graduated from The University of Western Ontario with a degree in Education in 2013. Currently I am completing my Masters of Counselling through The University of Calgary. I enjoy traveling, eating delicious food, and staying active. Most of all, I enjoy on connecting with my friends and family. I have always had the desire to help others, like my father, as he was a fire fighter who gave his life to rescue others. More than anything, I want to help others just as he did. I have had the incredible opportunity to work along side students and adults and guide them through their journey of healing and recovery. I plan to continue to guide and counsel others through a variety of counselling interventions that fit and work within the unique need of each individual.
