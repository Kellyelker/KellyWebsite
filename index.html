The Path to Counselling

Pathways

I have chosen the theme of pathways for my portfolio for a number of reasons. Embedded in my Existential beliefs is the core belief that individuals have choice in life. Regardless of circumstance we can choose the path we take: mentally, physically, spiritually, socially, the list goes on. The pathway that I have represented in this space is my pathway to lifelong learning, new career opportunities, and fulfilling my lifeâ€™s work of walking alongside others to improve and further their lives. This webpage covers three years of my life and my learning that have led me to a whole new path, that as a counsellor. It explores my learning through coursework, interactions with my peers, and practicum experience. Artifacts such as photos, quotes, excerpts from papers I have written, and commentary on what I have learned, as well as future goals will allow the reader to have a sense of what this pathway looked like for me.
