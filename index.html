Connections

I have chosen the theme of connections for my portfolio for a number of reasons. Connecting to yourself and others is a valuable and necessary part in the healing process. Connecting to our physical, spiritual, and mental and social lives allows us to live deeper and fuller, whether that be through joy and happiness or pain and suffering, being connected to these emotions allow for a deeper understanding of our own individual journey. Being able to connect with your most inner feelings, desires, and thoughts can offer powerful change into your journey through counselling. Connecting to your body, mind and soul will help you live a more balanced and harmonious life. I take on a holistic approach to counselling as I believe connecting with a variety of counselling interventions best fits in need of the client as they are the experts in their own lives. This portfolio explores three years of my counselling experience, including coursework, interactions with my peers, and practicum experience. I have also explored my counselling theory, special interests, and future directions. In addition, artifacts such as quotes, diagrams, and visual representations will be highlighted as well.
